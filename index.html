Welcome to Crazy Discounts Limited

We are a proudly family-run home furniture and appliances store nestled in the heart of North London at 17 Woodhouse Road, N12 9EN. What started as a passion for giving families access to quality home essentials at genuinely affordable prices has grown into one of the area's most trusted and talked-about stores.

Our carefully curated inventory spans both brand-new and high-quality pre-loved items — from plush beds and comfortable mattresses to the latest flat-screen TVs, reliable washing machines, stylish sofas, fridges, cookers, and everything in between. If your home needs it, chances are we've got it — or we can source it for you at a price that won't break the bank.

We believe every household deserves access to quality furniture and appliances without paying premium high-street prices. That's the Crazy Discounts promise — exceptional products, unbeatable value, and a personal touch that only a family-run business can offer. We also provide free local delivery, because getting your new purchases home should never be a hassle.

Everything For Your Home
