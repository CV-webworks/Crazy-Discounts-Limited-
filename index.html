Our Story

A FAMILY BUILT
ON VALUE

Crazy Discounts Limited was born from a simple belief — every family deserves a beautiful, comfortable home, regardless of budget. Based in the heart of North London at Woodhouse Road N12, we've spent over a decade building a reputation as the area's most trusted destination for quality furniture and appliances at prices that make a real difference.

We are a proudly family-run business. We understand what it truly means to furnish a home on a real budget — because we've lived it ourselves. That's why we stock everything from brand-new Smart TVs and beds to quality-tested washing machines, fridges, cookers, sofas and much, much more.

Our buying power and local knowledge mean we can source almost anything you need for your home — and deliver it fast to your door, completely free of charge. We've helped thousands of North London families turn houses into proper homes.
