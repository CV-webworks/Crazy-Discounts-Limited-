Welcome to Crazy Discounts Limited

We are a proudly family-run home furniture and appliances store nestled in the heart of North London at 17 Woodhouse Road, N12 9EN. What started as a passion for giving families access to quality home essentials at genuinely affordable prices has grown into one of the area's most trusted stores.

Our carefully curated inventory spans both brand-new and high-quality pre-loved items — from plush beds and mattresses to the latest flat-screen TVs, reliable washing machines, stylish sofas, fridges, cookers, and everything in between. If your home needs it, we've got it — or we can source it for you at an unbeatable price.

We believe every household deserves quality furniture and appliances without premium high-street prices. That's the Crazy Discounts promise — exceptional products, unbeatable value, and a personal touch that only a family business can deliver. Plus free local delivery.
