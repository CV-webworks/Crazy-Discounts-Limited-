Our Story

A FAMILY
BUILT ON VALUE

Crazy Discounts Limited was born from a simple belief — every family deserves a beautiful home, regardless of budget. Based in the heart of North London, we've spent years building a reputation as the go-to destination for quality furniture and appliances at prices that make a real difference.

We are a proudly family-run business. We understand what it means to furnish a home on a real budget — because we've been there ourselves. That's why we stock everything from brand-new beds and TVs to quality used sofas, washing machines, fridges, cookers and so much more.

Our buying power and local knowledge means we can source almost anything you need for your home — and deliver it fast to your door, for free. Over the years we've helped thousands of North Londoners turn houses into proper homes, without the financial stress.
